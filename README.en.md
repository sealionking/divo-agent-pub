## For your problem

Enterprises want AI Agents, but face three contradictions:

| Contradiction | What happens |
|---|---|
| **Capability vs. Compliance** | Smarter agents need more autonomy; enterprises need tighter boundaries |
| **Personalization vs. Consistency** | Teams want custom agent experiences; IT must keep them governable |
| **Speed vs. Sovereignty** | SaaS delivers fast but data leaves the perimeter; homegrown requires teams and time |

**DiVo** is built to resolve these contradictions.

---

## Why DiVo

### Sovereign governance
DiVo Agent Platform gives you full control over the agent runtime — data stays private, workflows are tailored on demand, tools and skills are governed by whitelists.

### Native Chinese LLM coverage
Deep integration with mainstream Chinese LLM providers (Baidu ERNIE, Alibaba Tongyi Qianwen, Tencent Hunyuan, Huawei Pangu, Zhipu GLM series, DeepSeek series, etc.) — self-hosted or Chinese sovereign models run under one unified governance plane.

### Enterprise identity onboarding
Plugs into your existing IdP (OIDC / SAML / CAS) — group and role inheritance stays intact.

### Open & bound Skill / MCP architecture
Skills and MCP Servers listed on DiVo's marketplace have gone through IT review; workspaces cannot establish outbound MCP channels on their own.

| Customer | Pain | DiVo value |
